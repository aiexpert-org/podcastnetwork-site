## How the quiz works

The scaffolded v0.2 deck runs 16 sample questions across 8 archetypes.
Roughly three minutes. You will pick a dominant and secondary archetype
at the end.

**The 8 archetypes:**

- **Concierge.** You make people feel taken care of.
- **Strategist.** You see the board and move pieces cleanly.
- **Analyst.** You go deep on the data before speaking.
- **Visionary.** You describe a future in enough detail that others help
  you build it.
- **Advocate.** You fight for the person or cause in front of you.
- **Storyteller.** You reach for the specific scene so the point actually
  lands.
- **Producer.** You move projects from talk into shipped work.
- **Guide.** You teach the shape of a problem so other people can solve
  it.

Everyone is a mix. Your dominant archetype shapes how you naturally
sound on the page, on the mic, and in a sales conversation. Once you
have your result, we tune every piece of copy PodcastNetwork.org writes
on your behalf to match it.
