## How the quiz works

95 questions, roughly 15 minutes. Five archetypes at the end:

- **Concierge** — you make people feel taken care of
- **Strategist** — you see the board and move pieces cleanly
- **Analyst** — you go deep on the data before speaking
- **Visionary** — you paint the future, then bring people to it
- **Advocate** — you fight for the person or cause in front of you

Everyone is a mix. Your dominant archetype shapes how you naturally sound
on the page, on the mic, and in a sales conversation. Once you have your
result, we tune every piece of copy PodcastNetwork.org writes on your
behalf to match it.
