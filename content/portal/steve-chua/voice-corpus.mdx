## Your voice corpus is being built

The Voice Corpus install runs in parallel to this portal. When it
finishes, this section renders your 23-dimension voice profile as an
interactive read. The PDF version ships alongside it in the Deliverables
Vault.

**What the analysis captures:**

1. Register (how formal versus how plainspoken)
2. Cadence (sentence length rhythm)
3. Density (ideas per paragraph)
4. Metaphor family (what your mind reaches for)
5. Certainty language (hedged versus declarative)
6. Emotional temperature
7. Question ratio
8. Signature verbs
9. Repeated phrasings
10. Structural moves you favor
11. Openings you gravitate toward
12. Closings you gravitate toward
13. Aside habits (parentheses, footnotes, dashes)
14. Rhetorical devices (contrast, tricolon, callbacks)
15. Pacing across a section
16. Distance from the reader (formal, intimate, coaching, peer)
17. Sensory anchors (sight, sound, kinesthetic)
18. Time-marker language (now, always, once, forever)
19. Point-of-view drift
20. Consistency across surfaces (page vs mic vs email)
21. Signature idioms
22. What you do when you disagree in print
23. What you do when you are certain
