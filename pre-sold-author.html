The Legacy JV

One price to us. The publish handled by Legacy.

What you pay

$30,000, one time, paid to The Podcast Network. That covers the pre-sell sequence and the manuscript production, start to finish. There is one price and one relationship to manage on your side.

How Legacy fits

Legacy Publishing handles publication and distribution under its standing structure: 10% of net book sales, with no separate upfront fee. Legacy is named as the publishing partner, and the royalty share is disclosed in the contract.

The two sides point at the same book from opposite ends of the timeline. We own the pre-sell and the audience. Legacy owns the publish and the long-term distribution. You get a pre-sold launch and a real publisher behind the finished book.

FAQ

The questions authors ask first.

Who is the package for?

Authors who have a defined audience or a clear path to one, who want a pre-sold launch rather than a cold release, and who want the book to start a longer relationship-engine play rather than be a one-off artifact.

What exactly does the $30,000 cover?

The pre-sell sequence and the full manuscript production: discovery, the podcast and relationship-engine arc, interview-based ghostwriting, a five-pass edit, cover and interior design, a production-ready manuscript, and a launch plan. Legacy handles the publish under the joint venture at 10% of net book sales with no separate upfront fee.

Do I have to write the book myself?

No. We ghostwrite the manuscript from your podcast catalog and source material so it reads in your voice. You show up for the conversations and the discovery work. We handle the writing, editing, and design.

What if I do not have a podcast yet?

That is fine. The pre-sell sequence is built around getting you in front of a defined audience, whether that starts from a show, a community, an organization, or from scratch. We map the right path in discovery.

How do I start?

Apply through the form on the home page. We review every application and follow up to schedule a discovery call.
